## 5. Features / Highlights  

### Business Problem  

Organizations often struggle to extract meaningful insights from raw financial datasets due to:
- Large volumes of unstructured or semi-structured data  
- Lack of centralized financial dashboards  
- Difficulty identifying trends, profitability, and cost drivers  

---

### Goal of the Dashboard  

The **Finance Analytics Dashboard** aims to:

- Transform raw Kaggle financial data into actionable insights  
- Provide a centralized view of financial performance  
- Identify revenue trends and expense patterns  
- Support budgeting, forecasting, and financial planning  
- Demonstrate Power BI and DAX proficiency  

---

### Walkthrough of Key Visuals  

#### Key Financial KPIs  
- Total Revenue  
- Total Expenses  
- Net Profit  
- Profit Margin (%)  
- Average Revenue per Period  

#### Revenue & Expense Trends (Line Chart)  
- Time-series analysis of revenue and costs  
- Helps identify growth patterns and seasonality  

#### Category-wise Financial Breakdown (Bar / Column Chart)  
- Revenue and profit comparison across categories  
- Identifies high-performing and underperforming segments  

#### Profitability Analysis  
- Net profit trends  
- Cost vs revenue comparison  

#### Time-Based Analysis  
- Monthly / yearly slicers  
- Dynamic filtering across all visuals
